Medford, MA Meetings

The Medford, MA Meetings podcast is intended to serve as a more easily downloadable version of what's served up on Medford Community Media's website.

  • Served up as a classic RSS feed: https://wnavarre.github.io/medford-meeting-podcast/meetings.rss
  • Online guides exist to help you add an RSS feed to your podcast app.
  • Spotify isn't supported, but plenty of podcast apps on Android and Apple are!
  • Audio-only to make downloading easier and less cumbersome.
  • Listen as you go for a walk or wherever podcasts fit into your lifestyle!
